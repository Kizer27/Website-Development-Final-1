Baldur's Gate 3

9.8/10

Description

Baldur's Gate 3 is turn based rpg based off Dungeons and Dragons. It gets its mechanincs and settings from DnD, causing it to feel very similar to the real thing. It is kniown for its length and depth, an average playthrough can reach 100 hours and still have plenty of undiscovered content. It has a large cast of characters and large open world, making it so you can play how you want to. You can play as a custom or premade character and you journey through the game world across three different acts.

Pros

  • Long
  • Dynamic
  • In-depth
  • Good Characters
  • Replayability
  • Expansive World
  • Graphics

Cons

  • Gameplay can Feel Repetitive
  • Can Feel Overwhelming
  • Custom Characters Lack Substansive Backstories
