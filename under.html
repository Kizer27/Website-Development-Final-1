Undertale

8/10

Description

Undertale is an indie rpg known for its characters and multiple endings. While the game is short and simplistic, it is a misleading great game. What differentiates Undertale from most rpgs in the combat system. On the player's turn, not only can they attack, they can also choose to show mercy on the monsters they're fighting. This is the basis for the branching, as who you choose to kill and spare has a major impact on how the game plays out.

Pros

  • Great Gameplay
  • Branching Story
  • Choices Matter
  • Good Characters
  • Good Humor

Cons

  • Very Short
  • Doesn't Make it Clear your Choices Matter Until it's too Late
  • Very Simplistic Graphics
