Fire Emblem Three Houses

8.5/10

Description

Fire Emblem Three Houses is a turn based strategy rpg for the Nintendo Switch. It is a part of the Fire Emblem series, but, like most Fire Emblem games, works as a standalone title. You play as a mercenary turned teacher and your students are the units you use in battle. This allows you to train them to fit whatever role you need on the battlefield. At the start of the game, you pick one of three houses to join, each with different students. While the first half of the game is similar no matter which house you pick, the second half is completely different.

Pros

  • Great Characters
  • Engaging Gameplay
  • Replayability
  • Solid Story
  • Lots of Side Content

Cons

  • Very Repetitive
  • A Lot of the Side Content is Dull
  • Many Characters are Pushed to the Side
